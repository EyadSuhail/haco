About HACO Co. Ltd.

Hamad Fahad Al-Awad Contracting Co. (HACO) was established in 1997 in Al-Khobar, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Since inception, our vision has been rooted in client value and satisfaction — a philosophy that has guided us through over two decades of consistent growth in the construction and electro-mechanical industries.

The company is led by Mr. Hamad Fahad Al-Awad as President and General Manager, supported by a team of highly experienced engineers and technical professionals with proven expertise in both project management and hands-on execution across Saudi Arabia's most demanding industrial environments.

We are an approved Sub-Contractor for Saudi Aramco and SEC, executing critical projects through leading main contractors including Zamil CoolCare, Energy & Power Contracting Co. (EPC), CCE & SICC, and Isam Kabbani.

  • Approved Sub-Contractor for Saudi Aramco & SEC
  • 15+ years executing Electro-Mechanical & HVAC projects
  • LSTK (Lump Sum Turnkey) project delivery capability
  • Qualified engineers, managers & skilled technicians on every project
  • Sole Agent for MEC SHOT India & ARMOR FIRE FZE UAE in Saudi Arabia
  • Operating across Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, and Maintenance sectors

Capabilities

Scope of Works

Our primary focus is executing Electro-mechanical & HVAC projects on a Lump Sum Turnkey (LSTK) basis, covering the complete scope of supply, fabrication, installation, and testing & commissioning of HVAC systems — fully complying with drawings, specifications, and applicable standards.

All projects are executed in strict compliance with SEC and Saudi Aramco standards. We assure our clients of absolute adherence to quality principles in the implementation and management of every project we commit to.

  • Fabrication & installation of HVAC duct and stainless steel ductwork
  • Air Handling Units (AHU), Air Cooled Condensing Units (ACCU), Air Cooled Water Chillers (ACWC)
  • Manual & Motorized Volume Dampers (MVD), Back Draft Dampers (BDD), Motorized Fire Smoke Dampers (MF/SD), Fire Dampers (FD)
  • Supply & Return Grilles and Diffusers
  • Light testing for duct networks and hydrostatic testing for chilled water pipes
  • Pre-commissioning and final commissioning with insulation and aluminum cladding
  • PVC pre-insulated pipe systems
  • Structural steel for industrial and commercial applications
  • Operation and maintenance of running projects
